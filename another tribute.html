A Tribute to Doctor D.K Olukoya

Doctor D.K Olukoya popularly known as DKO is a Nigerian Pastor, Author, Televangelist, Scientist. He was born 15th of july 1957 at Akure Ondo state where he did his secondary school; Methodist Boy's High school, Lagos, where he graduated with Grade One Distinction and was the best student in his set. In 1976 he gained admission into the prestigious University of Lagos, Unilag, where graduated from in 1980 with a first-class degree in Microbiology, the first and only from the department since the university was established 55 years ago.The passionate football lover then proceeded on a Commonwealth scholarship to the University of Reading, United Kingdom (UK) for his Doctorate degree in molecular genetics which he completed in record time, making him arguably the first Nigerian to obtain a Ph.D. in molecular genetics.

On his return to Nigeria, he was offered a juicy job at the Nigerian Institute;6 of Medical Research (NIMR), Yaba, Lagos wh;6ere he worked as a senior geneticist. He is said to have written more than 70 scientific papers before establishing the ‘Daniel and Fola Biotechnology Foundation’, a registered NGO, to train Nigerian citizens in molecular biology and biotechnology. Olukoya is believed to be first scientist to establish an indigenous Molecular Biology and Biotechnology laboratory in Nigeria. He was also a lecturer as well as external examiner to a number of Nigerian universities including the University of Lagos, UNILAG and the University of Benin, UNIBEN.

Doctor D.K Olukoya is known for his generousity toward the youth and footballers. He is a man of integrity and of good reputation.
